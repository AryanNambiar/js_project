Sudoku Solver

Sudoku is a popular number puzzle game that typically consists of a 9x9 grid divided into nine 3x3 subgrids or regions. The objective of Sudoku is to fill every empty cell in the grid with a digit from 1 to 9, ensuring that each row, each column, and each 3x3 subgrid contains all the digits from 1 to 9 without any repetition.
